Food Ordering App




One of the first projects I did while studying multimedia design was creating a food ordering app called My Pantry.

The concept of this app is that the customer writes what ingredients they have at home.
The app then suggests a recipe based on that.
The ingredients the customer dont have for the recipe they can order from the app and then get delivered to the door.

I came up with this concept to make cooking at home much more efficient.
This app was fully created in Adobe XD, the logo drawn by myself as well.

Moving Art Project




This was one of the more experimental projects I have done. The goal with this project was to create an animation using cut out pieces of an artwork.
I used Girl with a Pearl Earring by Johannes Vermeer.

The story of my animation is that she takes a picture of herself and then she turns into a cat, and then bawls her eyes out.
Dont ask me how i came up with that, because i literally have no idea.
My brain can be a scary place sometimes.

This project was created using only Adobe Photoshop and Animate, and was my first time using them.
Let me tell you...after i did this project Photoshop, Animate and me are not good friends.
